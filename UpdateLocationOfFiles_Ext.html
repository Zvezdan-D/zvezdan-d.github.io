After donating, if a day or two have passed and you still have not gotten my email, you should check your Spam folder; if you cannot find it in there, please send me an email with the information about your donation to: . Compatibility

There are two different enhanced versions of the add-on: the one that works only with MediaMonkey v2-4, and another that only works with MediaMonkey v5/20xx. The regular (non-enhanced) version of the add-on is available only for MM4!
